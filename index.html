About SEVA

SEVA (Software Ecommerce Value Added Services) aims to reach and empower small businesses and individuals through a pioneering digital platform with a vision to fulfil the dream of a digitally enabled India.

SEVA brings together under one roof - Innovators, Seva Saathi and Customers to support, be adaptive and unlock more opportunities to help them grow! SEVA empanels impactful, leading edge technology solution providers (Innovators) along with an influential, deep, pan India network of empowered, trainable growth specialists (Saathi) to enhance the support and business efficiency of our Customers.

Innovators

SEVA engages in promoting technology adoption for the empaneled Innovators who have developed technology to optimize business efficiency in the real world. Through SEVA, Innovators get direct access to a flexible sales workforce which directly impacts the consumer market. Empaneled on the SEVA platform are two unique Innovators; SEVA SHOP and GIBL.

To be an Innovator with SEVA, you need to register by filling in the Innovator Lead Form in the download section.

SEVA Saathi

SEVA provides earning, learning and community development opportunities to SAATHI’s (Individuals) who are tech literate individuals, housewives, budding entrepreneurs or just about anyone seeking a job.

SEVA empowers the SAATHI network through the SEVA mobile app to sell a host of solutions under one roof to consumers. SEVA SAATHI’s are given the platform to earn a handsome living, become influencers in their community and sharpen their skills through the SEVA Learning Management System for training and development.
